Complex Sentences - Lesson 8

Learn to form and use complex sentences in Amharic

New Vocabulary

እንደምን አደርክ/ሽ

endemen aderke/shi
How are you? (formal)
Listen to pronunciation

Practice

Choose the correct translation:

How are you?
Good morning
Thank you
Goodbye

Sentence Structure

Let's break down the components:

  • እንደምን - how
  • አደርክ/ሽ - you do/are (masculine/feminine)

Practice Sentence

እንደምን አደርክ፣ ዳዊት?

How are you, Dawit?
